📖 How to Play
✕
🎯 The Goal
Be the first to guess a secret number with 2 decimal places. The host sets the range — for example 1 to 1000. The system picks a random number inside that range.
🔄 Taking Turns
Players take turns guessing one number at a time. After each guess the system tells YOU privately if your guess is Too High or Too Low. Other players only see that you guessed — not your number or result.
🔒 Privacy is Key
Your guess results are completely private. Check the 📊 Board tab to see what others are doing. Check the 📜 History tab to see your own guess history. Nobody else can see your numbers!
⌨️ How to Guess
Use the number keypad at the bottom of the screen. Tap the numbers to build your guess, then tap the orange send button. On laptop you can also type with your keyboard and press Enter.
⏱ Timer
You have 60 seconds per turn. If your time runs out in solo mode, you will be asked if you want to continue. In multiplayer, your turn is skipped and passed to the next player.
🏆 Winning
The first player to guess the exact number wins the round! A celebration screen appears with confetti. The secret number is revealed to everyone.
🔄 Play Again
After a round ends, click Play Again. You can keep the same number range or set a new one. In multiplayer, all players must accept before the new round starts.
📺 Spectator / TV Screen
When creating a multiplayer game, enable the Spectator Screen option. This generates a separate TV code. Anyone who enters the TV code can watch the game live on a big screen without playing.
👤 Your Profile
Register once with your name and a 4-digit PIN. Your username is generated automatically. Your game history and medals are saved permanently. Login with your username and PIN on any device to access your stats.
