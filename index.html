📖 How to Play
✕
🎯 The Goal
Be the first to guess the secret number exactly. The number has 2 decimal places.
🔄 Taking Turns
Players take turns guessing. After each guess you are told if it is Too High or Too Low.
🔒 Privacy
You only see your own guess results. Opponents only see that you guessed — not what you guessed or whether it was high or low.
⏱ Timer
You have 60 seconds per turn. If you run out of time you are skipped.
🏆 Winning
The player who guesses the exact number wins the round!
